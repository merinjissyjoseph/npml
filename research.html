At NPML, our research focuses on the synthesis and development of nanoporous materials for adsorptive/membrane based separation. Our work focuses on understanding the gas adsorption and transport properties of various nanoporous materials. Currently our focus is on the following research areas:

    Materials

  • CNT
  • Graphene
  • Graphynes
  • Metal organic frameworks
  • Covalent organic frameworks

    Synthesis

  • Hydrothermal synthesis
  • Chemical vapor deposition
  • Interfacial polymerization

    Applications

  • Adsorption
  • Membranes
  • Gas separation
  • Carbon capture
  • Olefin/Paraffin separation
