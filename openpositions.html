Ph.D. Position

Due to the interdisciplinary nature of the work, candidates from diverse backgrounds like mechanical engineering, materials science, nanotechnology, chemistry, chemical engineering etc. are encouraged to apply for a Ph.D. position in our group.
  • Motivated young researchers who are interested to work in the field of nanoporous materials are welcome to apply for the MoE funded Ph.D. positions through the department. Please check the department website for more details.
  • Candidates with a valid scholarship (CSIR, DST-Inspire, UGC etc.) may apply throughout the year by dropping at email to deepu.babu@msme.iith.ac.in We also have active collaboration with chemistry department and co-supervision of Ph.D. dissertation is also possible.
