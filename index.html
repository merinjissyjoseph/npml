Welcome to the Nanoporous Materials lab at Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad. At NPML, we focus on the interdisciplinary research in the field of porous materials. Research at NPML is at the interface between materials science and chemistry involving synthesis, characterization and application of various nanoporous materials. Currently our focus is on carbon based nanomaterials (CNT, graphene, graphynes etc. ) and metal-organic frameworks for various separation applications.
