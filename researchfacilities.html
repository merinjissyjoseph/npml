High pressure gas adsorption setup

We are currently setting up a high-pressure volumetric gas adsorption setup which can measure adsorption isotherms over a wide range of temperature (R.T. - 80˚C) and pressure (0 - 30 bar) for various gases like CO2, N2, CH4 etc.


Permeation setup

We are also currently building a permeation setup for determining the gas permeation properties of membranes. The setup is designed to measure permeation of various gases like H2, N2, CO2, CH4, SF6 etc. over a wide temperature (R.T. - 200˚C) and pressure (0 - 10 bar) range.


Chemical vapor deposition setup

Using the seed grant money, we are also setting up a chemical vapor deposition setup for the synthesis of 2D materials. At first, the CVD setup will be optimized for the synthesis of high quality single layer graphene. The modularity of the setup ensures that the same setup can be later modified for the synthesis of other 2D materials.
